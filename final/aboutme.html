About

Hi! My name is Sarah, and I am the Creative Specialist behind Simply Tickled Shop!

While my husband was away, serving overseas, I needed a hobby to pass the time, so I began knitting! Eventually, people started asking me if I would sell my scarves, so I began building an inventory and vision for the shop. I use quality materials that ensure durability, while also selecting yarns that are soft and comfortable so they are practical and easy to wear everyday!

I'm all about cozy and stylish items, so I soon expanded my collection of scarves to include decorative throws. These over-sized, chunky knit throws are the perfect addition to any bed, sofa, bench, or chair.

When I'm not knitting, you will find me trying a new recipe in the kitchen, hiking new-found trails, taking a jog, or trying a new coffee shop!
