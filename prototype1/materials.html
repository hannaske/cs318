Quality Materials

Here in the Simply Tickled Shop, we use high quality yarn to ensure durability, while also selecting yarns that are soft and comfortable for practical every day use!

The yarn is 50% acrylic and 50% wool. It's a very soft, durable yarn - that won't leave you scratching!

Care: Spot treat, or hand wash gently in cool or lukewarm water and lay flat to dry.
